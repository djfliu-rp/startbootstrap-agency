PR

We are a FinTech Company, hiring for our Toronto office based near the Airport. We are looking for an individual with 3-5 years of Public Relations experience to join our team to assist with managing PR News Release, Media relationship.


What you will be doing:

    You will be responsible for the ongoing development of the global public relations strategy with a specific focus on the North American and Europe market.
    Attend conferences and industry events to develop and foster relationships with journalists.
    Work closely with the leadership team to organize interviews and gather quotes to successfully pitchmedia stories.
    Pitch media on a regular basis to ensure we have ongoing mentions.
    Write press releases and stories for media that support overall strategy and brand.
    You will be digging through our data trends to find stories to pitch media.
    Analyze and report media coverage.
    Look into conferences where we can speak and apply for opportunities.

Your experience must include the following:

    Fluency in English and Chinese Mandarin.
    Strong English and Chinese Speaking and Writing.
    3+ years in the public relations industry.
    A strong list of media contacts.
    Self-starter who is ready to dive in right away and start pitching.
    An understanding of data and how to use it to enhance media stories.
    Excellent writing and communication skills.
